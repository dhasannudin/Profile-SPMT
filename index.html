Who We Are
Non-Container
Port Operations
in Indonesia.

PT Pelindo Multi Terminal (SPMT) focuses on managing non-container port terminals, including liquid bulk, dry bulk, multipurpose, and vehicle terminals.

SPMT operates 33 port branches throughout Indonesia and three subsidiaries: PT Pelabuhan Tanjung Priok, PT Indonesia Kendaraan Terminal Tbk., and PT Terminal Curah Utama.

Vision

To be Indonesia's Connectivity Champion

Mission

To be a multipurpose terminal operator that provides ecosystem which creates added value for our stakeholders and country.
